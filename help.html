The input lag is too high or my screen is breaking a lot. Why is this so?

The problem is because of your 4G/Wifi network. On 4G, if you’re too far from the nearest cell tower, then your experience will be very poor. To find the closest cell tower to your location use the following options:
  • For Coverage in Your Area: http://www.coveragemap.trai.gov.in/
  • For Airtel: https://www.airtel.in/opennetwork/
