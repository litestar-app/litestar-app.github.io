• Basics
  • How much data does LiteStar use?

    LiteStar can consume between 200 - 300 MB of data an hour depending on the game

  • The input lag is too high or my screen is breaking a lot. Why is this so?

    The problem is because of your 4G/Wifi network. On 4G, if you’re too far from the nearest cell tower, then your experience will be very poor.

    To find the closest cell tower to your location use the following options:

    For Coverage in Your Area: http://www.coveragemap.trai.gov.in/
    For Airtel: https://www.airtel.in/opennetwork/

  • How to get more game time?

    Simply select the recharge amount in the recharge section and make a payment via PayTM

  • I’m seeing a blank screen in the speed test section. What do i do?

    This usually happens when we can’t connect to your network. It could be due to multiple reasons. Please write to us with your registered Mobile number and device name on help@litestar.app

  • Do you support any other form of payment?

    Currently we only support PayTM

  • How much bandwidth do I need to play on LiteStar?

    You need minimum 2 Mbps of bandwidth for decent gameplay

  • My game account got banned, what can I do about this?

    Sometimes game developers don’t recognize our systems and can possibly ban your gaming account. Unfortunately, we have no control over this. We can however reset the game for you on LiteStar and help you play again with another account or in Guest Mode

  • Which countries is LiteStar currently available in?

    Currently, we’re only available in India. If you would like us to be available in your country, please write to us at help@litestar.app

  • How do I get in touch with you?

    Just write to us with your registered mobile number and the issue you’re facing on the following email address: help@litestar.app
